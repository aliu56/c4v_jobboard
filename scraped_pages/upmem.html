About Us

UPMEM is an innovative semiconductor startup with a recognized global leadership in its field. His team is developing an “in-memory processor” solution, which accelerates data-intensive calculations and reduces their cost and energy footprint, in the datacenter and edge computing.

Founded in 2015, the company relies on an expert, entrepreneurial and international team based in Grenoble, as well as a global ecosystem both in terms of manufacturing and partners operating Processing In Memory (PIM).
We are growing strongly in order to accelerate the commercialization of our first and recent product internationally (United States, Asia, Europe, etc.) and continue our development.

UPMEM has leading industrial and financial investors from Europe, America and Asia reflecting the attractiveness of this award-winning technology.
We were selected among the 100 innovative companies in the Silicon 100 ranking by EETimes, and are one of the 12 Deep Tech winners in 2020 of the Global Innovation Competition phase 3, supported by the Investments for the Future Program, managed by the General Secretariat for Investment (SGPI) of the French government.
