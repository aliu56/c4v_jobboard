Job Vacancies

No search results matching your criteria!

We encourage all candidates to apply directly. If you are working with a recruitment agency and they are not on our preferred supplier list, then we may not be able to progress your application.

 

Recruitment agencies – please do not send us unsolicited CVs if you are not on our preferred supplier list. If you do so, we reserve the right to contact the candidate directly and your T&Cs will not apply.

 

We use the personal information you submit to assess your suitability for the role, to conduct testing and interviews, to support you with any access requirements or reasonable adjustments to make a decision about your appointment.

 

We store your data in our applicant tracking system, called Greenhouse. We keep the personal information of unsuccessful candidates for six months after the end of the relevant recruitment cycle.We never share your personal data with other organisations and will only share it within Graphcore with employees involved in the hiring process for the role you are applying for.

 

For our full candidate privacy policy, please see: https://www.graphcore.ai/privacy For any queries, please contact: peopleteam@graphcore.ai

 

We will not ask you to interview, share salary details or make job offers over social media channels and all of our recruiters have a name@graphcore.ai email address.
