Who we are

Our mission and our values

Everli was founded in 2014 with the aim of making grocery shopping simple, fast and convenient . Clients choose their favorite supermarket and do their grocery online: then, a personal Shopper goes to the store to carefully make the grocery and deliver it straight at home.

We want to change the grocery experience and give back all the time and efforts that everyone now spends in picking, queuing and bringing his own groceries at home. We want to do this to allow people to spend their time on what really matters: life.

The company is now a multi-milion well funded scale-up with top Italian and international investors, strategic partnerships and talented staff of more than 200 people.
